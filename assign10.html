Chapter 25

It is possible to specifically design and offer print friendly versions of your pages for users who want to print a copy for offline reading. Similar to Google Maps. CSS makes it easy to create pages that will change appearance based on how they are viewed. Some web pages are print=friendly already. Pages with dark backgrounds, dynamic links, and several images might prove to be unwiedly for the average printer. A printed page is typically 8 and 1/2 by 11 inches, whereas a screen can vary.
